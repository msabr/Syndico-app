Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome to the FAQ section. Here you'll find clear answers to our most common questions.

Where is my job posting advertised?

Syndico advertises job postings across multiple platforms to maximize visibility. Your job posting will be shared on our main website, social media channels, and partner networks. Additionally, we use targeted advertising to reach the most relevant candidates based on location, skills, and experience level.

What Makes Your Business Plans So Special?

Our business plans are tailored to meet the unique needs of property managers and residents. We offer flexible pricing, comprehensive features including online payments, maintenance tracking, document management, and 24/7 customer support. Plus, our plans scale with your needs.

Reset Password With Phone Number?

Yes, you can reset your password using your registered phone number. Simply click on "Forgot Password" on the login page, select "Reset via SMS", and enter your phone number. You'll receive a verification code via SMS that you can use to create a new password.

How do I redeem a coupon?

To redeem a coupon, go to your account settings and select "Billing & Payments". Click on "Apply Coupon Code", enter your coupon code in the designated field, and click "Apply". The discount will be automatically applied to your next payment.

How long will it take to post my job?

Job postings are typically reviewed and published within 24 hours of submission. Once approved, your posting will remain active for 30 days or until you close it. You'll receive an email notification when your job goes live.

What is your cancellation policy?

You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. If you cancel, you'll continue to have access to all features until the end of your current billing period. No refunds are provided for partial months, but you won't be charged for subsequent periods.

Where Can I Find Market Research Reports?

Market research reports are available in the "Resources" section of your dashboard. Navigate to Dashboard > Resources > Market Reports. You'll find monthly reports on property trends, rental rates, and market analytics specific to your region.

Do I need to know PHP to use TheDocs?

No programming knowledge is required to use Syndico. Our platform is designed with an intuitive interface that anyone can use. However, if you want to integrate with other systems using our API, basic technical knowledge would be helpful. We provide comprehensive documentation and support for all integration needs.

How soon will I start receiving resumes?

You typically start receiving applications within 24-48 hours of your job posting going live. The response rate depends on factors like job type, location, and compensation. You'll receive email notifications for each new application, and can view all submissions in your dashboard.
