Frequently Asked Questions

How can I pay my monthly charges?
You can pay your charges through multiple methods: Credit Card, Bank Transfer, PayPal, or Cash at the office. Simply go to "My Charges" section and click "Pay Now" on any pending charge.
What happens if I miss a payment deadline?
Late payments may incur a penalty fee. We recommend paying before the due date. If you're facing financial difficulties, please contact the syndic office to discuss payment plans.
How do I book a shared facility?
Navigate to "Book a Space", select your desired facility, choose a date and time slot, and submit your request. Some facilities require admin approval which typically takes 24 hours.
Can I cancel my reservation?
Yes, you can cancel reservations up to 24 hours before the scheduled time. Go to "My Reservations" and click the cancel button. Cancellations made less than 24 hours in advance may not be refundable.
How long does it take to resolve a complaint?
Resolution times vary based on priority and complexity. Urgent issues are addressed within 24 hours, while standard complaints typically take 3-7 business days.
How do I update my contact information?
Go to "My Information" in your dashboard, click "Edit Profile", update your details, and save changes. For security reasons, email changes require verification.
I forgot my password. What should I do?
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email address, and you'll receive a password reset link. The link is valid for 1 hour.
